Weather App

Built with html and css

Welcome to Weatherly, where we bring meteorological marvels to your fingertips! In the hustle and bustle of daily life, knowing the weather forecast is not just a luxury but a necessity. Enter Weatherly, your reliable and intuitive weather companion designed to deliver accurate, real-time weather updates in a visually appealing and user-friendly manner. Key Features: Real-Time Weather Updates: Stay in the know with up-to-the-minute weather information for your location or any place around the globe. Our app fetches data from reputable sources to ensure accuracy. Intuitive User Interface: Navigating through Weatherly is a breeze. The app boasts a clean and intuitive design, making it easy for users of all ages to access the information they need without any hassle. Detailed Forecasts: Get a comprehensive outlook with hourly and daily forecasts. Plan your day or week with confidence, armed with detailed insights into temperature,
